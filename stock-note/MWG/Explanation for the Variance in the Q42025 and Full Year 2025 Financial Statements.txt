Based on Clause 4, Article 14 of Circular No. 96/2020/TT-BTC dated November 16, 2020, by the Ministry of Finance guiding the disclosure of information on the stock market, an explanation of the cause is required when:

After-tax profit in the Business Results Report of the reporting period changes by 10% or more compared to the same period last year.

After-tax profit in the reporting period is a loss, or shifts from a profit in the same period last year to a loss in this period or vice versa.

After-tax profit in the reporting period shows a variance before and after audit or review of 5% or more, or shifts from loss to profit or vice-versa.

Mobile World Investment Corporation would like to explain the change of over 10% between the Q4/2025 and full year 2025 Financial Statements (FS) compared to the same period as follows.

Explanation for the Consolidated FS for Q4/2025 and Full Year 2025
Consolidated after-tax profit for Q4/2025 reached 2,086.5 billion VND, an increase of 145% compared to 852.1 billion VND in the same period.

Consolidated after-tax profit for the full year 2025 reached 7,075.5 billion VND, an increase of 90% compared to 3,733.3 billion VND in the same period.

Causes:

MWG's net profit in 2025 recorded strong growth driven by (i) consolidated net revenue increasing by 16.4%, helping to significantly improve absolute gross profit, (ii) the company continuing to maintain operational discipline and optimizing costs across the entire chain system, and (iii) effective network expansion.

MWG recorded an after-tax profit of 7,075.5 billion VND in 2025, exceeding the full year plan of 4,850 billion VND by 46%, reflecting a strong improvement in operational efficiency across core business segments.

For the thegioididong.com and Dien May Xanh chains, 2025 revenue reached 105.7 trillion VND, up 18% over the same period, despite the average number of stores decreasing by about 100. Growth came from existing stores with an increase of over 20%, reflecting a clear improvement in operational efficiency. These two chains continue to hold a key role in the Group's profit structure. This result comes from a strategy focusing on core activities, enhancing customer experience, and promoting omni-channel retail and flexible financial solutions.

The Bach Hoa Xanh chain recorded 2025 revenue growth of 14% compared to 2024, with steady growth momentum maintained in Q4. Alongside revenue growth, the chain recorded a profit for the second consecutive year, with the improvement level being many times higher than the previous year, thereby increasing its contribution to the Group's consolidated profit. This result comes from a strategy of maintaining healthy revenue growth, promoting online channels, effectively expanding the store network, and simultaneously strengthening loss control and optimizing store costs and operational efficiency.

Other chains including An Khang, Avakids, and Erablue all showed clear improvements in operational efficiency compared to the same period, contributing to the overall profit growth of the Group.

Explanation for the Separate FS for Q4/2025 and Full Year 2025
After-tax profit of the parent company in Q4/2025 reached 5,349.4 billion VND, an increase of 227% compared to 1,637.6 billion VND in 2024.

After-tax profit of the parent company in 2025 reached 8,852.9 billion VND, an increase of 237% compared to 2,628.3 billion VND in 2024.

Cause: The Parent Company received income from dividends and other financial activities in 2025.
