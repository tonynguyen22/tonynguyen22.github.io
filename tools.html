ValuWise

Professional Equity Valuation Platform

Launch App

A browser-based equity valuation tool that walks through a complete fundamental analysis workflow — from DCF modeling with custom WACC assumptions, to comparable company analysis (EV/EBITDA, P/E, EV/Sales multiples), to an automated financial health score that grades a company across profitability, leverage, and growth dimensions.

Designed to mirror the workflow of a buy-side analyst: input a ticker, pull financials, stress-test assumptions across bull/base/bear scenarios, and arrive at a price target with supporting documentation. Built as a standalone web app with no server-side dependencies.

Tonivest Research

Personal Investment Research Platform & Portfolio Tracker

View Site

This website — built from scratch as a personal investment research platform. Houses in-depth equity research reports (DCF, comparable analysis, investment thesis) across US and Vietnam markets, alongside a live portfolio tracker that fetches real-time prices via the Finnhub API and visualizes sector allocation using Chart.js.

Reports follow an institutional format: business summary, industry context, competitive advantages, financial analysis, valuation model, and final verdict with price target. Designed to serve as a working demonstration of equity research skills.
